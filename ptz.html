Keyboard shortcuts

Arrows = pan/tilt, +/- = zoom, Ctrl/Cmd + +/- = focus, 1/2/3 or Numpad 1/2/3 = preset recall, Shift+1/2/3 = preset save, Numpad 0 / 0 = home. Hold Alt for fine steps. P = toggle preview drag/wheel overlay. M = mirror preview, R / Shift+R = rotate preview, F = stage mode toggle. A = reset autofocus. Ctrl/Cmd+M = mirror remote, Ctrl/Cmd+R = rotate remote, Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+R = reset remote rotation. Use the camera recovery card to refresh the sender camera or request its available camera list without opening the director UI. Use "Invert pan" or "Invert tilt" if your camera responds opposite to expected direction.
