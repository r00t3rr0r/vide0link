    Auto Face Crop Help

    Auto Face Crop attempts to keep your face centered in the webcam overlay during preview and recording.

    1. Turn on Webcam.
    2. Enable Auto Face Crop (Exp).
    3. Click Prepare Sources, allow camera access, then choose your display source.

    If Auto Face Crop is disabled, your browser likely does not expose FaceDetector for this page. Use a current Chromium browser for the best chance of support.
